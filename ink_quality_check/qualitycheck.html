Ink Quality Check:

Ink quality checking is crucial to ensure that the ink used in printing processes meets the required standards for performance and reliability. This process involves evaluating various parameters such as viscosity, drying time, adhesion, gloss level, and pH. By maintaining high ink quality, businesses can achieve superior printing results, reduce waste, and improve overall efficiency.







Brief Descriptions of Parameters:

Viscosity (cP): Measures the thickness of the ink.

Drying Time (seconds): Time taken for the ink to dry.

Adhesion (%): Percentage of ink that adheres to the surface.

Gloss Level (%): Measures the shine of the ink surface.

pH: Measures the acidity or alkalinity of the ink.
